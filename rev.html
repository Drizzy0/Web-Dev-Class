REPRODUCTION

Reproduction is the fundamental biological process by which living
organisms produce offspring, ensuring the continuation of a species. It
occurs through two primary methods: sexual reproduction, involving the
fusion of male and female gametes for genetic diversity, and asexual
reproduction, where a single parent creates genetically identical clones.
